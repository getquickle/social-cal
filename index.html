        hooks · the first frame is the hook

        1scale. a small figure against a huge wall of mountain or desert.
        2stillness that breaks. hold a quiet frame, then frost cracks or a runner enters.
        3detail first. open on salt on skin or frost on a sleeve, then pull back to the world.
        4sound cold open. the pour, footsteps, or wind before any image lands.
        5one subject, much space. a lone figure, or the pouch, in a field of empty.

        most quickle, least used: detail first plus a sound cold open. it stops the scroll without trying.
